# Banana Prompts Optimizer Skill

## 🌟 Overview
Banana Prompts is an essential skill and toolkit for generative AI workflows. It helps digital artists, marketers, and developers overcome "writer's block" in prompt engineering by providing reverse-engineering capabilities and a massive database of inspiration.

## 🚀 Core Capabilities

* **Image-to-Prompt Analysis:** Reverse-engineers any reference image to extract exact keywords for style, lighting, composition, and rendering engines.
* **Premium Prompt Library:** Access thousands of verified, high-quality prompts optimized for Midjourney v6 and Stable Diffusion.
* **Workflow Optimization:** Instantly transform vague ideas into structured, professional AI art prompts.
